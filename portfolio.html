Overview:

Master of Digital Media (MDM) program at the Center for Digital media is a special program that aims to improve communication skills of students and professionals that are in creative industries through improvisation. Venture Compass

The multi-day adventure planning app Design

Main App Interfaces:
Main Features:
  • Free-roam
  • Trip planner
  • Equipment Check-list
  • Tracking
  • Emergency
Overview:

Venture Compass is an app which helps with overall preparation by providing users with clear yet flexible trip plans that help them prepare rest stops and destinations along the way. The equipment suggestor helps less experienced users prepare for trips by providing trip-specific equipment based on environment and expected weather.

User Journey Map Illustration
Feature Descriptions

Milestone tracking is the most important feature, so my idea about this feature is accomplished by having the app track the user’s trip through the various stops they make along the way. As the user journey map i designed shows above. Once a trip is finished, users can review it and see all the places they’ve been. Along the way, they’ll be given the estimated time remaining on their trip. If they ever decide to venture off-course, Venture Compass will help guide them back when needed, and adjust the time left to their destination. We can continue implementing danger signals for hikes the users would come across during their trip. I think when they tap on a hiking area, key information such as difficulty level, danger precautions, and hike duration will all be given at a glance. A trail map with danger areas will also be provided. Services can be communicated via campsite information cards, where things such as washrooms, showers, and available food can be viewed.

Descriptions and Process

Humanity In-Dependence consists of a single screen projection of an image that can change based off of audience interaction.

The audience can interact with the artwork by shining a flashlight at the light sensor located inside the grey box container. Upon detecting the flashlight, the screen will switch back and forth between that of a human face and a robotic face. The challenge I met was the projected image was initially a static image. I should make the image to be more dynamic prior to the prompt of the flashlight so that the artwork is less static when seen from a distance. To solve the problem, I re-programmed in the processing of the image. so that it would fade back and forth between the two images via a timer.The audience can continue manipulate the image for as long as they have the flashlight aimed at the light sensor or until they decide to leave. The project requires a person to input a light source in reality which will manipulate the digital space through a computer. I found that the primary visual feedback given by digital image manipulation requires both human and machine working together for a successful interaction to take place.
