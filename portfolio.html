Descriptions and Process

The topic for the project:

The effectiveness of improvisation in developing communication and collaborations skills among teams in an emerging multicultural world.

Overview:

Master of Digital Media (MDM) program at the Center for Digital media is a special program that aims to improve communication skills of students and professionals that are in creative industries through improvisation. We are focusing on this special method to improve communication skills and its lecturer/designer Dr. Pennefather. Our goal is presenting the effectiveness of this class and specifically, Ball Game and other games help students to improve their team building and communication skills. And how these games prepare students to meet UX clients. The primary audience is students and professionals who will be inspired by the program and specifically the games. We want to express improvisation is a fun and great way to improve their communication skills in the creative industry.

Project problems:

The beginning of storyboard is interesting but missing some connecting scenes between lecture and games.The colors scheme is stated and there are pictures of the exterior of Center for Digital media as establishing shots. I have made a dramatic change due to some issues regarding in-class footage so even though preproduction have provided a general guideline and it has explained what the main point of interest of what we want to focus on, the actual representation of the project is different compared to preproduction materials.

Final edit:

The final edit has included motion graphics, fixed audio and corrected colors and it has basically represented our final ideas regarding this projects. The changes I have suggested in the rough cut review have been included and realized. There should be some changes regarding the choice of fonts and illustration style in motion graphics. I met some challenges such as thinking the idea of the motion graphics sequence because it should be more creative to match the voice-over and the transition in each scene. Overall Zhihan have been investing his time and utilize his skills to make the perfect contribution to the final results of the project.

User Journey Illustration
Overview:

Venture Compass is an app which helps with overall preparation by providing users with clear yet flexible trip plans that help them prepare rest stops and destinations along the way. The equipment suggestor helps less experienced users prepare for trips by providing trip-specific equipment based on environment and expected weather.

Feature Descriptions

Milestone tracking is accomplished by having the app track the user’s trip through the various stops they make along the way. Once a trip is finished, users can review it and see all the places they’ve been. Along the way, they’ll be given the estimated time remaining on their trip. If they ever decide to venture off-course, Venture Compass will help guide them back when needed, and adjust the time left to their destination. We can continue implementing danger signals for hikes the users would come across during their trip. When they tap on a hiking area, key information such as difficulty level, danger precautions, and hike duration will all be given at a glance. A trail map with danger areas will also be provided. Services can be communicated via campsite information cards, where things such as washrooms, showers, and available food can be viewed.
