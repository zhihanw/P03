Improvise and Play Documentary

Documentary Location: Centre For Digital Media

The topic:

The effectiveness of improvisation in developing communication and collaborations skills among teams in an emerging multicultural world.

Moodboard
CDM

Descriptions with Process

Overview:

Master of Digital Media (MDM) program at the Center for Digital media is a special program that aims to improve communication skills of students and professionals that are in creative industries through improvisation. So I am focusing on this special method to improve communication skills and its lecturer/designer Dr. Pennefather. My goal is presenting the effectiveness of this class and specifically, Ball Game and other games help students to improve their team building and communication skills. And how these games prepare students to meet UX clients. The primary audience is students and professionals who will be inspired by the program and specifically the games. In the documentary, I want to express improvisation as a fun and great way to improve their communication skills in the creative industry.

Project initial problems:

The beginning of storyboard is interesting but missing some connecting scenes between lecture and games.The colors scheme is stated and there are pictures of the exterior of Center for Digital media as establishing shots. Then I have made a dramatic change due to some issues regarding in-class footage so even though preproduction have provided a general guideline and it has explained what the main point of interest of what we want to focus on, the actual representation of the project is different compared to preproduction materials.

Initial Storyboards

Initially，the documentary will begin with several interviews with montage editing of current students, who come from different countries, speaking totally random words of phrases in their native dialect. It attempts to attract audience’s attention and curiosity by different language to describe their feeling at the beginning. After that, it continues with the medium close up shot for interview of current students with their names on the screen, to describe the program completely. It appeals the beginner level of understanding about how improvisation class improves them. During the interview, it will be some footage from the class to flash in, and keeps audience getting interest about the progress rather than stay with same scene. Later, there will be Dr. Pennefather’s voice over on introduction of his class and his way of teaching to differentiate the speciality of his teaching style. Use existing footage to convey the fun and exciting atmosphere of the class. It should be energetic and make people feel they should be part of this experience.

Venture Compass

The multi-day adventure planning app Design

Main App Interfaces:
Main Features:
  • Free-roam
  • Trip planner
  • Equipment Check-list
  • Tracking
  • Emergency
Overview:

Venture Compass is an app which helps with overall preparation by providing users with clear yet flexible trip plans that help them prepare rest stops and destinations along the way. The equipment suggestor helps less experienced users prepare for trips by providing trip-specific equipment based on environment and expected weather.

User Journey Map Illustration
Feature Descriptions

Milestone tracking is the most important feature, so my idea about this feature is accomplished by having the app track the user’s trip through the various stops they make along the way. As the user journey map i designed shows above. Once a trip is finished, users can review it and see all the places they’ve been. Along the way, they’ll be given the estimated time remaining on their trip. If they ever decide to venture off-course, Venture Compass will help guide them back when needed, and adjust the time left to their destination. We can continue implementing danger signals for hikes the users would come across during their trip. I think when they tap on a hiking area, key information such as difficulty level, danger precautions, and hike duration will all be given at a glance. A trail map with danger areas will also be provided. Services can be communicated via campsite information cards, where things such as washrooms, showers, and available food can be viewed.
